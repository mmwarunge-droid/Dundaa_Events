# Dundaa Payment and Commission Guide

## Accepted Channels

- M-Pesa
- Bank
- Card

## Gift Split Example

Gift amount: KES 10,000

- Tax/platform fee at 20%: KES 2,000
- Net amount: KES 8,000
- Platform commission at 10% of net: KES 800
- Influencer receives: KES 7,200

## Cashout Threshold

Minimum cashout: KES 1,000
