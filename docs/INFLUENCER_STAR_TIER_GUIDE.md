# Dundaa Influencer Star and Tier Guide

## Tiers

- Nano: 20 equivalent 5-star reviews
- Mega: 50 equivalent 5-star reviews
- Super: 100 equivalent 5-star reviews

## Conversion Rules

- 10 three-star ratings = 1 equivalent five-star
- 5 four-star ratings = 1 equivalent five-star
- 1 five-star rating = 1 equivalent five-star

## Decay Rule

Star value expires after 3 months for active tier calculations.

## Purpose

The decay model encourages fresh, consistent engagement.
